The 3rd Annual Pineapple GP is happening May 2 2019. This year our chosen charity is Jigsaw, a great organisation looking out for the mental health of young people in Ireland.

There’s a couple of ways to join in, we’ve made it super easy.

Sign up to be one of the drivers below, a minibus will go from the city centre to the karting and from there back to the city centre for the party. Or join in by simply donating, you’ll automatically be entered into the raffle. Drive or donate you are invited to the afterparty.
