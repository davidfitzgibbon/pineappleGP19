Last year was great fun so we’re doing it again, Pineapple GP 2019 is happening March 23rd 2019. Our chosen charity is Jigsaw, a wonderful organisation looking out for the mental health of young people in Ireland.

There’s a couple of ways to join in.

Choose DRIVE below to be part of the on-track action, or choose DONATE to give any amount and be in with a chance to win a Modello Dare Experience worth €400.

Either way, you are invited to the afterparty.

Deets

  • Where: Kylemore Karting ( regular busses and Luas from the city )
  • When: 3pm, March 23
  • Cost: €20

Afterparty: TBC from 6pm
