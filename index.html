The 3rd Annual Pineapple GP is happening March 23rd 2019. This year our chosen charity is Jigsaw, a great organisation looking out for the mental health of young people in Ireland.

There’s a couple of ways to join in, we’ve made it super easy.

Drive to join in the on track action, donate any amount to automatically be in with a chance to win a Modello Dare Experience worth €400. Either way, you are invited to the afterparty.

Deets

  • Where: Kylemore Karting ( regular busses and Luas from the city )
  • When: March 23rd - 3pm
  • Cost: €20
  • Afterparty: TBC from 6pm
